about us

history

The property forgery issues are widespread and the original owners of the property are not able to get justice sometimes,even after such a good judiciary system. The true owners wait for a long time for his own resources and sometimes even end losing their assets.

vision

We at Prop regi are trying to make a transparent system for handling problems like land forgery using the Blockchain Technology.This will solve such issues in no time , even reduce the probability of such incidences to happen.

goals

Our Goal is to make the most transparent and consistent property registration system in our country, and awaring people all around about such a technology that preserves the time of judiciary which is being wasted in handling issues like land occupying,land forgery etc.
